# 4. Episodic vs Non-episodic

An environment is **episodic** if the agent’s experience is divided into episodes.  
Quality of action depends just on the episode itself.

**Examples**  
- chess → non-episodic  
- chest x-ray analyzer → episodic

Episodic environments are much easier because planning is limited to within an episode.

# Properties of Environments

### 4. Episodic vs Non-episodic
An environment is episodic if the agent's experience is divided into episodes.
* episode - consists of the agent perceiving and acting

Quality of action depends just on the episode itself since subsequent episodes do not depend on actions in previous episodes.

Episodic environments are much easier to deal with because planning is limited to within an episode.
* chess - non-episodic
* chest x-ray analyzer - episodic

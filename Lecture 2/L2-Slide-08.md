# Autonomy

### Agent and Environment
* Cambridge Dictionary: the ability to make your own decisions without being controlled by anyone else
* AI: the ability to adapt to its environment; flexibility

If an agent relies solely on its built-in knowledge and completely disregards the environment, the agent lacks autonomy.
* clock - no autonomy

**A good rational agent acts according to**:
* built-in knowledge - often imperfect or partial
* own experience (percept sequence)

A system is autonomous to the extent that its behavior is determined by its own experience

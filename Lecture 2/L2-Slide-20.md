# Properties of Environments

### 1. Fully vs Partially Observable

**Fully observable environment** if agent's sensors give access to the complete state of the environment at each point in time
* sensors detect all aspects of environment that are relevant to the choice of action
* relevance depends on performance measure
* agent need not maintain internal state to keep track of the world

**Partially observable environment**
* part of state is not measured by sensors
* noisy, inaccurate sensors

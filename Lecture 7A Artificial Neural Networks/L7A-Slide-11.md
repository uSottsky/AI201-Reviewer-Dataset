# Biological Neurons

### Short History of ANN
* **1943:** McCulloch and Pitts proposed the first computational models of neuron
* **1949:** Hebb proposed the first learning rule
* **1958:** Rosenblatt's work on perceptrons
* **1969:** Minsky and Papert - on the limitations of neural networks
* **1970s:** Decade of dormancy for neural networks
* **1980-1990:** Neural network returns (self-organization, backpropagation, etc)
* **1990s-2010s:** Neural network becomes mainstream
* **2012 - Present:** Deep Learning

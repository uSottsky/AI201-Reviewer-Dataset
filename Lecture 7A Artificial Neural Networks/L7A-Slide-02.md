# Biological Neurons

![[Pasted image 20260316025531.png]]
> Reference: Slide 2 - Diagram of a biological neuron detailing the Dendrites, Cell body, Nucleus, Axon hillock, Axon, Myelin sheath, Synaptic terminals, Synapse, and the signal direction from the Presynaptic cell to the Postsynaptic cell.

**Neuron:** the fundamental cellular unit of the nervous system consisting of a cell body, dendrites, and axon

**Major Structures**
* **cell body** - contains the nucleus and the various organelles
* **dendrites** - thread-like branching structures of a neuron that conduct nerve impulses towards the cell body
* **axon** - long thread-like structure that carries the nerve impulses generated by the cell body to other neurons

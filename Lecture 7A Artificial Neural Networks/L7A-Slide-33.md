# Biological Neurons

* **Class A:** union of regions mapped onto vertices 000, 001, 011
* **Class B:** regions for other vertices (010, 100, 110, 111)

**Not possible:**
* Class A: 000, 111
* Class B: others

*Note: vertex 101 does not correspond to any of the polyhedral regions (virtual polyhedra).*

A two layer perceptron can separate classes each consisting of unions of polyhedral regions but not any arbitrary union of such regions.

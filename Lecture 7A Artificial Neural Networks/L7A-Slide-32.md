> Reference: Slide 32 - A 3D cube (hypercube in 3 dimensions) with vertices labeled with 3-bit binary codes (000 to 111). A shaded plane cuts through the cube, separating some vertices from others.

Output neuron realizes another hyperplane that separates the hypercube into 2 parts such that some vertices are on one side and others on other side.

Output neuron provides the MLP with the capability to classify vectors into classes consisting of unions of polyhedral regions.

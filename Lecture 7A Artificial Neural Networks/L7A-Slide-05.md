# Biological Neurons

### Brain vs Computer

**Speed** - neurons are much slower than silicon gates
* events in silicon chip happen in the nanosecond range
* neural events occur in the millisecond range
* slow operation of the individual neurons is more than compensated for by the brain's massive parallelism

**Energy Efficiency**
* computer - $1$ microwatt per operation
* brain - $10^{-10}$ microwatt per operation

**Programmability**
* brain has the ability to build its own rules through experience (hardwiring of the mammalian brain) which is the basis of learning
* for humans, hardwiring takes place most dramatically during the first two years from birth during which one million synapses are formed per second

**Brain Plasticity** - ability of the developing nervous system to adapt to its own environment.
Two mechanisms involved in brain plasticity:
1. creation of new synaptic connections
2. modification of existing synapses

### Multilayer Feedforward Networks

![[Pasted image 20260316030333.png]]
> Reference: Slide 21 - Diagram of a Multilayer Feedforward Network. Shows an Input layer connected to a First hidden layer, connected to a Second hidden layer, finally connected to an Output layer. All layers are fully connected to the next.

* has one or more hidden layers of neurons (hidden = not connected to the "outside world" through the neuron's output)
* fully connected network - every node in each layer is connected to every other node in the adjacent forward layer.

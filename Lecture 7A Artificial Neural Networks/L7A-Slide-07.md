# Biological Neurons

### Brain and ANN
The brain's ability to learn is based on:
* the way the neurons are connected to the sensory and other neurons
* the neuron's capacity to store information in its synapses

The Artificial Neural Network (ANN) is:
* a machine inspired by the massively interconnected neurons in the brain
* implemented in hardware or software or both
* capable of learning

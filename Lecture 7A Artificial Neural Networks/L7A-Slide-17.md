### The Two-Layer Perceptron

![[Pasted image 20260316030157.png]]
> Reference: Slide 17 - 2D graph showing the XOR points with TWO decision lines ($g_1(x)$ and $g_2(x)$) drawn to create a bounded region (shaded gray) enclosing the Class A points, separating them from the Class B points.

* $2$ decision lines $\rightarrow$ $2$ perceptrons
* **2 Phases**
  1. calculate position of feature vector $x$ with respect to each decision line
  2. find position of $x$ with respect to both lines

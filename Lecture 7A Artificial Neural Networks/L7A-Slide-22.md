# Biological Neurons

### Partially Connected Multilayer Feedforward Networks

![[Pasted image 20260316030355.png]]
> Reference: Slide 22 - Diagram showing a Time signal sampled at discrete intervals feeding into an Input layer, which connects to a Layer of hidden neurons, and then an Output layer. The connections are partial (receptive fields) rather than fully connected.

* **receptive field of a neuron:** the set of localized nodes feeding a neuron
* receptive fields are common in networks that deal with time series inputs
* time series information is represented all at once as a spatial pattern over the input layer

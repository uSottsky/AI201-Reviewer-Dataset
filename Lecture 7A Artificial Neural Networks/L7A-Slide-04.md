# Biological Neurons

![[Pasted image 20260316025559.png]]
> Reference: Slide 4 - Diagram of two connecting neurons highlighting the Presynaptic cell sending a signal across the synapse to the Postsynaptic cell.

* The transmitting cell is called a **presynaptic cell** and the receiving cell is called the **postsynaptic cell**.
* A single neuron may receive information from numerous neighboring neurons via thousands of synapses, some of them excitatory and other inhibitory.
* If the summation of the excitatory and inhibitory signals exceeds a certain threshold, the neuron "fires" or generates a signal which is transmitted along its axon.

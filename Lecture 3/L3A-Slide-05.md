# Problem Solving Agent

### The 8-Puzzle
Consists of a $3\times3$ board with eight numbered tiles and a blank space.

![[Pasted image 20260314131050.png]]
* **objective**: reach the goal state
* simpler to think of "blank space moves left" than "move tile 4 into the blank space"
* 8-puzzle and its larger version, the 15-puzzle, are the standard test problems for new search algorithms in AI
* solving the 24-puzzle is very challenging even for the best search algorithms developed so far

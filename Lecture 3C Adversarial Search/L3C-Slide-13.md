# Mini-Max

### Chess
* game of perfect information / played on an $8\times8$ checkered board
* initial state: white corner to the right / white queen on white, black queen on black / rooks on corner squares, then two knights, two bishops / second row: fill up with pawns
* **pawn:** can only move forward. For the first advance, it has the option of moving one or two squares forward but thereafter may move only one square at a time. Capture diagonally but only forwards.
* **rook:** move row-wise or column-wise to any distance unless blocked
* **bishop:** move diagonally to any distance unless blocked
* **knight:** L-movements
* **queen:** row-wise, column-wise, diagonally to any distance unless blocked
* **king:** one step at a time
* all pieces except pawn capture in their normal direction of movement
* not obligatory to capture your opponent
* **objective:** capture opponent's king

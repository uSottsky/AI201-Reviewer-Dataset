# Game Playing in AI

* Board games are interesting because they offer pure, abstract competition.
* Less abstract games, such as robot soccer, are beginning to attract more interest in AI.
* Game playing is an idealization of worlds in which hostile agents act so as to diminish one's well-being.
* **Usefulness of games in AI:** games help us better understand how to act in complex and uncertain domains.

**Types of games studied in AI:**
* games of perfect information (knowledge available to each player is the same)
* games of imperfect information

perfect information = fully observable
move = action
position = state

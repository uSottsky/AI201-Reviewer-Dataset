# Go

>![[Pasted image 20260315005900.png]]
> Reference: Slide 26 - A 19x19 Go board populated with black and white stones.

* game of perfect information played on a $19\times19$ board with stones of two colors
* **objective:** place stones so that they surround those of your opponent
* best move is selected more on the basis of how the position "looks" than on intricate tactical analysis
* difference between two Go players can be measured precisely (i.e., Go player who beats another by a certain margin in one game is very likely to beat the other player by a nearly identical margin in subsequent games)

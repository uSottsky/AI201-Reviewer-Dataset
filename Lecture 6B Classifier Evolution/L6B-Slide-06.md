# Precision, Recall, and F1-Score Example
![[Pasted image 20260316023035.png]]
$$Precision (P) = \frac{TP}{TP+FP} = \frac{23}{23+5} = 0.821$$

$$Recall (R) = \frac{TP}{TP+FN} = \frac{23}{23+12} = 0.657$$

$$F_1 = 2 \cdot \frac{P \cdot R}{P+R} = 2 \cdot \frac{0.821 \cdot 0.657}{0.821+0.657} = 0.730$$

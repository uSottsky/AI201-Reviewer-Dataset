# History of AI

Excellent resource: The History of Artificial Intelligence (https://exhibits.stanford.edu/ai)

### Inception (1943-1956)
* 1943: McCulloch and Pitts - computational model of the neuron
* 1950: Minsky and Edwards - SNARC (3,000 vacuum tubes) - first neural network computer
* 1950: Turing - Turing Test; programs that learn rather than hard-coded intelligence; warned that achieving AI might not be the best thing for the human race
* 1952: Strachey and Samuel - first checkers-playing programs
* 1956: Dartmouth College AI Workshop - widely considered as the founding event of AI; first mention of Artificial Intelligence by McCarthy
* 1956: Simon's Logic Theorist proved mathematical theorems

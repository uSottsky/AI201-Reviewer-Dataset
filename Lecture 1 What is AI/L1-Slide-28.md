# Five Laws of Intelligent Action

### Grand Challenges of AI
1 Translating Telephone
* Japanese speaker converses with an English speaker in real time.
* requires a large vocabulary; must be capable of translating unrehearsed, continuous speech;
* requires a natural-sounding speech synthesis that preserves the speaker characteristics; NLP system must deal with ambiguity, non-grammaticality, and incomplete phrases

2 Accident-Avoiding Car
* requires advances in vision and sensor fusion (of vision, laser, sonar, etc. sensors)
* obstacle detection and avoidance capability

# History of AI

### Dose of Reality (1966-1973)
overconfidence due to promising performance of systems on simple examples (e.g., microworld)

* theorem proving - failed to prove theorems involving more than a dozen facts
* Minsky and Papert - perceptron cannot learn the exclusive or operation
* Lighthill Report (1973) - emphasized failure to come to grips with combinatorial explosion
* AI Winter triggered by Lighthill Report (UK) and ALPAC Report (US) - reduced funding for AI research

# History of AI

### Neural Networks and Probabilistic Reasoning (1986-present)
* 2011: IBM Watson can answer questions posed in natural language; competed on Jeopardy and won first prize of $1M against human champions; commercial application - decisions in lung cancer treatment at Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center
* 2012: Krizhevshy's AlexNet - started Deep Learning
* 2014: Goodfellow's Generative Adversarial Network
* 2015: ResNet152 exceeded human performance for ImageNet
* 2016: AlphaGo's victories over human players
* 2017: Self-Supervised Learning
* 2017: Google Brain's Transformer Architecture

# LDA and QDA

For data described by a Gaussian:
* **Bayesian linear classifier (Linear Discriminant Analysis)** - equal covariance matrices
* **Bayesian quadratic classifier (Quadratic Discriminant Analysis)** - unequal covariance matrices

**LDA and QDA**
* large number of training parameters (large training data required)
* **(+)** good performance due to decision surfaces that produce a reasonably good partition of the feature space
* **(+)** good performance also due to good statistical properties associated with Gaussian models (e.g., bias-variance tradeoff)

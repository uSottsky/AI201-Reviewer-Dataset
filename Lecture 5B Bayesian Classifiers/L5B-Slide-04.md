# Supervised vs Unsupervised Learning

## Pattern Recognition Problems

1. **Supervised Learning**: Given $(x^1, y^1), \dots, (x^N, y^N)$ where $x^i \in \mathbb{R}^l$ are the datapoints and $y^i$ is the class label or value, choose function $f$ such that $f(x) = y$
   - Classification: $y^i \in$ finite set
   - Regression: $y^i \in \mathbb{R}$ or $y^i \in \mathbb{R}^d$

2. **Unsupervised Learning**: Given $x^1, \dots, x^N$ where $x^i \in \mathbb{R}^l$ find patterns in the data
   - Clustering
   - Density Estimation
   - Dimensionality Reduction

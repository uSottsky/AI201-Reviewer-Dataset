# Pattern Recognition

**Goal:** classification of data into a number of categories (classes)
* data = image, video, audio signals, text, etc.

**Pattern Recognition** - integral part of machine intelligence systems
* **Machine Vision:** analysis of images or videos captured by camera to produce description of what is imaged
* **Character Recognition:** classify sets of pixels into the correct letter, number, symbol or punctuation classes
* **Computer-Aided Medical Diagnosis:** classify lab results (x-ray images, EEG, etc) into disease classes
* **Speech Recognition:** classify speech signals into text

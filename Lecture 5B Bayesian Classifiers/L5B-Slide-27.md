# Decision Lines Visualization

![[Pasted image 20260315204709.png]]
> Reference: Slide 27 - Vector plot showing decision lines for normally distributed vectors. It marks the midpoint $x_0$ and shows the perpendicular bisector for equiprobable classes, alongside a shifted line when probabilities differ.

Decision lines for normally distributed vectors with $\Sigma=\sigma^{2}I$. 
For $P(\omega_{j})=P(\omega_{i})$ the decision line (black) is the perpendicular bisector of the line segment connecting the two means.

When $P(\omega_{j})>P(\omega_{i})$ the decision line (blue) is closer to $\mu_{i}$.

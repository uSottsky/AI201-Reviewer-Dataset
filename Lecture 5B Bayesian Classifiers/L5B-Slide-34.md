# Estimation of Unknown Probability Density Functions

Assumed so far that pdfs are known. In most cases pdfs are unknown and must be estimated from data.
Sometimes type of pdf is known (e.g., Gaussian) but parameters are unknown.

**Methods**
1. Maximum Likelihood Parameter Estimation (MLE)
2. Maximum A Posteriori Probability Estimation (MAP)
3. Bayesian Inference
4. Maximum Entropy Estimation
5. Mixture Models
6. Non-Parametric Estimation

# History of AI

### Neural Networks and Probabilistic Reasoning (1986-present)
* 1980's: Hidden Markov Models successful in speech recognition
* 1982: Vapnik and Chervonenkis - contributions to Learning Theory; VC dimension
* 1986: Rumelhart, Hinton and Williams - Backpropagation Algorithm
* 1988: Pearl's Bayesian Networks
* 1988: Sutton's Reinforcement Learning
* 1992: Vapnik's Support Vector Machine

# Five Laws of Intelligent Action

### The Five Laws of Intelligent Action
1. Bounded Rationality Implies Opportunistic Search
* computational constraints placed on intelligent action forces the intelligent agent to just aim for a "good enough" solution
* when intelligent agents operate under conditions that overload computational resources, opportunistic strategies and tactics of "optimal least computation search" rather than "optimal shortest path search" are employed.

Research focus on approximate algorithms of optimal least computation search

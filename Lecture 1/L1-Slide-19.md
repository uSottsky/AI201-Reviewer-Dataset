# AI Problem Domains

### AlphaZero (DeepMind, 2017)
* chess, Go, Shogi
* Kasparov - it was a pleasure to watch AlphaZero play, especially since its style was open and dynamic like his own
* Nielsen - AlphaZero's played like a superior alien species

DeepMind: "The game of chess represented the pinnacle of AI research over several decades. State-of-the-art programs are based on powerful engines that search many millions of positions, leveraging handcrafted domain expertise and sophisticated domain adaptations. AlphaZero is a generic reinforcement learning algorithm originally devised for the game of go - that achieved superior results within a few hours, searching a thousand times fewer positions, given no domain knowledge except the rules."

# Definitions of AI

### The Cognitive Modeling Approach
* introspection - analyze actual workings of the human mind
* psychological experiments - observe person in action
* brain imaging / fMRI / EEG- observe the brain in action, "read the mind"
* come up with a testable theories of the human mind

### The Laws of Thought Approach
* based on Aristotelian logic - uses deductive reasoning to arrive at conclusion (e.g.,modus ponens, modus tolens)
* syllogism - a formula of argument consisting of two propositions (premises), and the conclusion that is logically drawn from them
    * "All men are mortal" (major premise)
    * "Socrates is a man" (minor premise)
    * "Socrates is mortal" (conclusion)
* syllogisms always give correct conclusions provided premises are correct
* formal logic provides a precise notation for describing correct reasoning
* concerned with how to obtain correct inferences

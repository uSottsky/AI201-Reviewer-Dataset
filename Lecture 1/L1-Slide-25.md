# Five Laws of Intelligent Action

### The Five Laws of Intelligent Action
3. The Magic Number is $70,000\pm20,000$
An expert knows $70,000\pm20,000$ "chunks" of information. This number measures the size of an expert's knowledge base.
* supported by experimental evidence in cognitive science (e.g. vocabularies of college graduates is about $70,000\pm20,000$)
* knowledge base of expert systems grows towards tens of thousands It has been observed that no human being reaches world-class expert status without at least a decade of intense full-time study and practice in the domain.
* even the most talented do not reach expert levels of competence without immense effort
* there is only enough time to be an expert in only two or three other areas of our lifetime

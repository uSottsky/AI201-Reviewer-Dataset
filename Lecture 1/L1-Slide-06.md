# Definitions of AI

To pass the modern version of the Turing Test, a machine must have the following capabilities:

* **Natural Language Processing** - ability to communicate successfully in a human language
* **Knowledge Representation** - represent and store knowledge in a symbolic or non-symbolic way
* **Automated Reasoning** - draw conclusions from available knowledge using induction or deduction
* **Machine Learning** - detect and extrapolate from patterns and learn to adapt to new situations
* **Computer Vision / Speech Recognition** - perceive the world
* **Robotics** - manipulate objects and move about in the world

These 6 subfields are the major disciplines within AI.

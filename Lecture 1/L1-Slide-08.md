# Definitions of AI

### The Rational Agent Approach
* acting rationally means acting so as to achieve one's goals, given one's beliefs
* beliefs = truths about the world

**AIMA's Definition of AI:** AI is the study and construction of rational agents
* forming correct inferences is one way of acting rationally = reason logically to the conclusion that a particular action will achieve one's goals and then act according to that conclusion
* in many situations, there is no provably correct action to perform and yet it is necessary to act.
* "Part of intelligence is knowing what to do when one does not know what to do"
* making correct inferences is just part of rationality

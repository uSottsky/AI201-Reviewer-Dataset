# AI Problem Domains

### Robotics
robot: an active, artificial agent whose environment is the physical world
* Computer-controlled manipulators are routinely used today in manufacturing environments

Autonomous mobile systems
* e.g. robotic vehicles - for navigation on roads
* challenging since they require several disciplines such as vision, advanced sensors, high-speed processors, planning, control, and learning
* Navlab V - a commercial van from General Motors modified for autonomous steering - navigated correctly 98% of the time from Washington DC to San Diego, CA in1995.
* When in doubt, the system asks the human driver to take over
* 2010: VisLab - first intercontinental land journey completed by autonomous vehicles; Parma, Italy to Shanghai, China in 100 days
* 2019: Waymo (2.3M km), General Motors (1.2M km), Pony.ai (281K km), Zoox (107.8K km)

### Multilayer Perceptron (MLP)
* MLP can be viewed as a nonlinear input-output mapping.
* network's continuous nonlinear activation functions produce continuous nonlinear output functions endowing it the capacity to perform interpolation and generalize from training inputs to unknown ones.
* training involves searching over a large weight space corresponding to the large set of possible functions
* training is equivalent to looking for the function that will fit a given training data set.

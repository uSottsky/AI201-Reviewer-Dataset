# Bernoulli Distribution

Toss a coin once. Let $X \in \{0,1\}$ be the binary random variable with probability of "heads" as $\theta$. This describes a Bernoulli distribution $(X \sim Ber(\theta))$ defined as:

$$
Ber(\theta) = \begin{cases} 
\theta & \text{if } x=1 \\ 
1-\theta & \text{if } x=0 
\end{cases}
$$

or

$$Ber(\theta) = \theta^{\mathbb{I}(x=1)}(1-\theta)^{\mathbb{I}(x=0)}$$

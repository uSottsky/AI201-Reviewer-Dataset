# Models

Models are of the form:
* $p(y|x)$ (supervised learning)
* $p(x)$ (unsupervised learning)

### Parametric vs Non-Parametric Models

**Parametric Model**
* fixed number of parameters
* **advantage:** fast
* **disadvantage:** stronger assumptions regarding nature of distributions
* **example:** logistic regression

**Non-Parametric Model**
* number of parameters grow with amount of training data
* **advantage:** more flexible
* **disadvantage:** computationally intractable for large datasets
* **example:** KNN

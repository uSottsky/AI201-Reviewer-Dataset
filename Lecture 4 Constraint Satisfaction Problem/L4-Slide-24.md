### Tree-structured CSP
General CSPs: worst-case time is $O(d^{n})$



>![[Pasted image 20260315015417.png]]
> Reference:   A generic tree-structured constraint graph with nodes A through F.


**Theorem:**
If the constraint graph has no loops, the CSP can be solved in $O(n \cdot d^{2})$ time.
* also applies to logical and probabilistic reasoning
* an important example of the relation between syntactic restrictions and the complexity of reasoning

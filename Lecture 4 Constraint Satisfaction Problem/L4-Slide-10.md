# What is CSP?

### CSP Domains
* **discrete, finite domains** - map coloring, scheduling with time limits, 8-queens
* **continuous domains** - scheduling of Hubble Telescope experiments

### CSP constraints
* **unary constraint** - single variable not allowed to take on certain values
* **binary constraint** - two variables together not allowed to take on certain values
* **higher-order constraint** - ternary, quaternary, etc. constraints
* **global constraint** - arbitrary number of variables (e.g. Alldiff in Sudoku)

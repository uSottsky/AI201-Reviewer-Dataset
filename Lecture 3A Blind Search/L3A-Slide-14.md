# Problem Types

### Exploratory Problem
* agent is ignorant of the effects of its actions and experiments in the real world to gain experience which it will use to solve subsequent problems.
* The agent must experiment gradually discovering what its actions do and what sorts of states exist.
* Newborn babies face the task of handling exploratory problems.

**Reinforcement Learning** attempts to solve exploratory problems:
* multi-armed bandit problem
* exploration vs exploitation dilemma

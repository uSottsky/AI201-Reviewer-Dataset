# Problem Types

### Contingency Problem
In a contingency problem the correct action sequence depends on a possible contingency that might arise during execution.

* **Example**: car driving - contingency problem since even if we know how to get to our destination, traffic conditions, accidents, etc. could alter our planned route
* **Games** - contingency problems

Contingency problems require interleaving of search and execution.

# Blind Search: Depth-First Search

### 3. Depth-First Search
* **DFS** expands one of the nodes at the deepest level of the tree.
* When the search hits a non-goal node with no expansions, it goes back and expands nodes at shallower levels.
* implemented with a queuing function that puts the newly generated states at the front of the queue

# Linear Separability

![[Pasted image 20260315214535.png]]
> Reference: Slide 8 - Two scatter plots. The left shows data perfectly separated by a straight line (linearly separable). The right shows overlapping clusters (not linearly separable).

* **linearly separable:** data points can be completely separated by a linear decision boundary.
* **not linearly separable:** data points overlap in such a way that no single straight line can divide the classes.
